NDBI: 
Retrieved from a Sentinel 2 Image from 20210905, the closest cloud free image to the study day
Represents the Normalized Difference Built Index, the normalized difference between B11 and B8 on Sentinel 2

NDVI: 
Retrieved from a Sentinel 2 Image from 20210905, the closest cloud free image to the study day
Represents the Normalized Difference Vegetation Index, the normalized difference between B8 and B4 on Sentinel 2 

ESA:
Represents a Land Cover Dataset from the European Space Agency from 2020 at 10m resolution. See additional Information here:
https://developers.google.com/earth-engine/datasets/catalog/ESA_WorldCover_v100

NLCD:
Represents a Land Cover Dataset from the USGS from 2016 at 30m resolution. See additional information here:
https://developers.google.com/earth-engine/datasets/catalog/USGS_NLCD_RELEASES_2016_REL

LST:
Represents Land Surface Temperature from 3 cloud free images close to study area from Landsat 8. From B10. Values are in Kelvin with a scale and offset as indicated here:
https://developers.google.com/earth-engine/datasets/catalog/LANDSAT_LC08_C02_T1_L2

NAIP:
On Google Drive due to size
Represents bands from NAIP imagery at 1m resolution. See more information here:
